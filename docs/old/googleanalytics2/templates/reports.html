About Reports

Generate various types of reports to analyze your Google Analytics 4 data. Each report type provides different insights and visualizations.

Report Types

  • URL Analytics: Analyze performance metrics for a specific URL, including traffic sources, user behavior, and device information.
  • Property Aggregation: Aggregate data across multiple GA4 properties to get a comprehensive view of your analytics data.

Report Features

  • Customizable Metrics: Select the metrics you want to include in your report.
  • Customizable Dimensions: Choose dimensions to segment your data.
  • Date Range Selection: Specify the time period for your report.
  • PDF Export: Download reports as PDF files for sharing or archiving.
  • Data Visualization: View your data in charts and graphs for better insights.
